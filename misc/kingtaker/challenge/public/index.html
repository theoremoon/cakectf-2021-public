How to Play?

Use arrow keys to move yoshiking, find the crown for him!
The left bottom counter indicates the remaining moves he can make.
Be aware that the box consumes 2 moves per push.

FAQ

Q. I can't solve the puzzle. Any hint?
A. Unfortunately I'm not smart enough to solve it too...

Q. Is this game inspired by any existing games?
A. Not at all.

Q. Why is yoshiking green in this game?
A. The original icon of yoshiking was a green dino without the crown.
